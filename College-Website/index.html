Courses We Offer

Swarnandhra College of Engineering and Technology offers three of the best technical courses for students

B.Tech

The B.Tech course is a full-time four-year (eight semesters) Bachelor's Degree in Technology. The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Technology and Engineering.

M.Tech

The M.Tech course is a full-time two-year (four semesters) Master's Degree in Technology. The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Technology and Engineering.

Diploma

The Diploma course is a full-time three-year (six semesters) program in Engineering. The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for a career in Engineering and Technology.
